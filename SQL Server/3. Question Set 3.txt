Transaction
-Transaction helps us to implement series of activities as one Logical Unit.
-Either everything is successfull or everything rollback.

Inner Join
-Inner Join selects matching records from both table.

Left Join
-All the records from the left table selected and only matching records from the right table.

Right Join
-All the records from the right table and only matching record from the left table.

Full Outer Join
-All matching and unmatching records from both left and right table are selected.

Cross Join
-It is a cartesian product.
-No 'on' clause is used.
-Every record of left table will be mapped with every record of right table(cartesian product).
